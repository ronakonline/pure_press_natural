Description

Groundnut De-Oiled Cake (DOC) is a high-quality protein-rich by-product obtained after the extraction of oil from peanuts. It is widely used as an excellent animal feed ingredient due to its balanced nutritional profile and digestibility. Processed under strict quality control, our de-oiled cake is free from harmful additives and ensures consistent nutritional value for livestock health.

Key Features

  • Rich source of protein and essential amino acids
  • Supports healthy growth and improved milk yield in cattle
  • High digestibility and palatability for livestock
  • Processed under hygienic conditions to maintain freshness
  • Economical and sustainable animal feed option

Applications

  • Used as cattle, poultry, and aquaculture feed
  • Can be blended with other feed ingredients for balanced nutrition
  • Valuable for dairy farms, poultry farms, and feed manufacturers

Supplied in 50kg bags, Pure Press Naturals Groundnut De-Oiled Cake offers reliable quality and nutrition, making it a trusted choice for farmers and feed industries worldwide.
