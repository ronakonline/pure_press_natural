Description

Our peanut oil is made from carefully selected groundnuts, gently pressed and refined to preserve its natural golden color, light aroma, and rich taste. The process begins with high-quality peanuts that are cleaned, pressed, and filtered under strict food safety standards. This ensures a 100% pure, premium oil free from additives.

  • Naturally cholesterol-free and trans-fat-free
  • Rich in heart-healthy monounsaturated fats
  • A source of vitamin E, an antioxidant that supports skin and immune health
  • High smoke point, making it a safer choice for high-heat cooking

Usage

  • Perfect for deep-frying, stir-frying, grilling, or light frying
  • Adds a mild, nutty flavor that complements both traditional and modern recipes
  • Ideal for restaurants, catering, and everyday family cooking

With a balance of taste, nutrition, and performance, Pure Press Naturals peanut oil is your trusted partner for healthy and delicious meals. We manufacture in 1L, 5L, 15L, and 20L retail packs, as well as bulk flexitanks for commercial buyers.
